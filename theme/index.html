About us

FK Holding is a global investment firm with a diversified portfolio spanning private and public equity across sectors such as real estate, power generation, manufacturing, hospitality, oil & gas, and financial services. As a financial investor, FK Holding expands its global reach, while Al Khor Holding manages its assets. We specialize in real estate, trading, oil, gas, and more, offering investment opportunities for both long-term growth and quick returns, ensuring stability and growth for our investors.
